Admissions

Join our inclusive academic community. We welcome applications from both Korean and international students.

Eligibility & Age

Grade placement is based on birth year, following the official age chart.

Enrollment

Pre-K to Grade 8 | Rolling admissions | Entry: February & August

Mid-Year Admissions Policy

Students applying mid-year will be tested for placement one grade level above their intended entry. If the student does not meet the admission criteria, they will not be eligible to reapply for the same grade in the following school year.
