Education at ACA

Empowering students through creativity, critical thinking, and global readiness.

Primary Level

At ACA, our commitment to offering a curriculum that is consistently one to two grade levels above the standard ensures that students are challenged academically and prepared for future success. This advanced approach allows students to engage with more complex content and develop critical thinking skills at an accelerated pace.

By exposing students to higher-level material earlier, they gain confidence in tackling difficult concepts, which enhances their problem-solving abilities and deepens their understanding. The benefits of this approach extend beyond academics, fostering resilience, perseverance, and a growth mindset—traits essential for excelling in both Korean and international high school and university settings.

Core Subjects
  • Science, Technology, Engineering, Math (STEM)
  • ELA – Critical Reading and Writing
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science & Digital Citizenship
  • Art / Music
  • Sports
  • Spanish as a Foreign Language
  • Korean Language, History, and Society
Core Program Features
  • Accelerated Academics: Students are 2 years ahead in grade level, following rigorous standards.
  • Maker Space Integration: Practical, project-based learning using the ACA Science and Engineering Lab fosters innovation and design thinking.
  • ESL Support Program: Students below grade-level English proficiency attend a structured ESL program to build confidence and skills.
