Education at ACA

Empowering students through creativity, critical thinking, and global readiness.

Secondary Level

ACA Institute of Science and Technology (IST) equips middle and high school students with essential 21st-century skills through a STEAM-integrated, inquiry-based learning framework that prioritizes critical thinking, creativity, and real-world problem-solving.

By aligning our flexible curriculum with the latest global education trends, students are exposed to dynamic, hands-on experiences that deepen their understanding and spark innovation. This approach not only strengthens technical and academic foundations but also nurtures individual strengths and learning styles, guiding each student along a personalized career track toward success in today’s fast-evolving, technology-driven world.

Academic Focus

ACA IST offers an interdisciplinary, globally relevant curriculum built to develop problem-solving and research skills through advanced academics and real-world application.

Student Support and Development

Students benefit from immersive mentorships and professional experiences led by industry experts. This helps ignite curiosity and inspires innovation by connecting learning to real-world impact.

Program Features
  • Personalized Learning Plans
  • In-House STEM Competitions (Open to students around Korea)
  • Global Competitions & Programs
  • Online Learning Programs
  • Internship Opportunities
  • Higher Education Preparation
  • Social Innovation Projects
  • Mentorships – Connecting students to experts in fields of interest
