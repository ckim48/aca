Headmaster’s Welcome

CEO - Irene G.S. Lim

Welcome to ACA International. I am delighted to share with you the unique and transformative education we provide to our learners. ACA is an institution founded on the vision of cultivating global leaders, and we are committed to nurturing the potential of every student who joins our school community.

At ACA International, our program is carefully designed to advance academic excellence while fostering intellectual curiosity, character development, and emotional well-being. Our rigorous, bilingual curriculum not only ensures that students are challenged academically but also equips them with the critical thinking and problem-solving skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic, interconnected world.

Through inquiry-based learning and project-based approaches, our students explore real-world challenges, encouraging creativity, collaboration, and innovation. We are proud to offer an environment where students are encouraged to discover their unique talents and interests.

Our dedicated faculty are passionate educators who celebrate the diversity of our students, fostering a safe and inclusive space where every child feels valued and inspired to achieve their personal best. On our beautiful campus, students engage in a holistic educational experience that balances academic rigor with enrichment opportunities in the arts, physical education, and technology.

At ACA, we believe in the boundless potential of children and strive to support their growth into confident, compassionate, and responsible future leaders. As role models, our teachers embody the values of respect, curiosity, and perseverance, instilling in our students the skills and mindset to build a brighter future.

We look forward to partnering with you on this exciting journey of discovery and learning. Together, we will empower your children to achieve greatness and take pride in their academic and personal growth.

Principal - Mr. Justin Marlowe

Modern education demands a careful balance between innovative teaching methods and enduring educational goals—the cultivation of curiosity, diligence, and discipline. To help students thrive, I work closely with our teachers to ensure every classroom is rooted in respect, creating an environment where learning flourishes.

By supporting educators in applying evidence-based strategies, I ensure students not only master core academic fundamentals but also pursue interests that will distinguish them in a competitive world. Under my leadership, students build strong foundations for lifelong success, excelling academically while developing bold, ambitious visions for their futures.
