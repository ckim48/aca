Belonging at ACA

Our Mission

ACA International builds a community where talented students thrive through holistic education, developing into global citizens prepared to shape the future.

Learning Goals

  • Cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and resilience.
  • Foster bilingual proficiency and global awareness.
  • Promote lifelong learning through inquiry and innovation.

Core Values

Curious Thinkers

Ask questions, explore ideas, and aim to understand how the world works.

Global Citizens

Respect differences, care for the world, and make it better through action.

Creative Problem-Solvers

Use imagination, explore solutions, and learn from mistakes.

Effective Communicators

Express ideas clearly, listen respectfully, and collaborate effectively.

Empathetic Leaders

Lead with kindness, empower others, and promote inclusion.

Resilient Learners

Persevere through challenges and grow from experience.

Accreditation & School Improvement

ACA is accredited by Cognia, reflecting our commitment to excellence through ongoing innovation and improvement. We:

  • Continuously refine our curriculum to meet modern needs.
  • Invest in teacher development for high-impact instruction.
  • Leverage data and feedback to enhance learning outcomes.
